7. Teks digambar :" Pertanyaannya : Identify the language features ! How is each paragraphs organized ? ✓ Harus pake penjelasan


READING COMPREHENSION

A. Identify the language features !

~ It uses the simple present tense

~ It uses the simple past tense when telling the past history.

~ It uses the spesific participant

~ It uses the descriptive adjective to clarify the object

~ It uses the relating verb (copula)

B. How is each paragraphs organized!

~ The beginning paragraph is callled an identification or introduction.

~ The further paragraph is called a description or body paragraph.

~ The last paragraph is called a conclusion.

EXPLANATION

A Descriptive text or descriptive essay is a kind of composition which describes the certain objects. It can be people, animals, things, and places. A descriptive text/essay presents a comprehensive picture through five senses rather than the subjective matter.

Generic structure of a descriptive text :

1) Identification [Introduction]

Identification is kind of an introduction of an object that we're talking about. It completely tells the information of what it is, where it is, which will be depicted in the text.

"The Panama Canal is an artificial waterway in the Central American country of Panama that connects the Atlantic and Pacific Ocean. "

2) Description [A body paragraph]

A Description tells more about the spesific characters, surroundings or the story.

"It is only 82 kilometres long. If you go around South America by ship then you need to travel another 15,000 kilometres. So the canal saves a lot of travel time. It takes around 8 to 10 hours to cross the canal.

The French started building the canal in 1881, but they couldn't finish it. The project was started again in 1904 by the United States and the canal was finally finished in 1914. Many people died while they were building the canal, some say up to 25,000. For the rest of the 20th century, the United States controlled the canal, but gave control back to Panama in 2000."

3) Conclusion

A conclusion contains the summary of the paragraph.

"Every year, around 40,000 ships come through the canal. These are mostly commercial ships. They transport goods for trade between Asia and America, or Europe. In 2016 the government of Panama made the canal bigger, so that now 99 per cent of ships can pass through it."

Language features of descriptive text :

1) It uses the simple present

Exp.

~ around 40,000 ships come through the canal.

~ The canal saves a lot of travel time.

~ It takes around 8 to 10 hours

~ They transport goods for trade between Asia and America.

2) It uses the relating verb (Copula)

Exp.

~ The Panama Canal is an artificial waterway

3) It uses the specific participant/certain object.

Example : the Panama Canal

4) It uses the descriptive adjective

Example : bigger

28. Is there ............... bones in the fridge to feed two hundred dogs?​


Jawaban:

Is there (ENOUGH) bones in the fridge to feed two hundred dogs?​

Penjelasan:

Enough artinya sebagai cukup. Enough menggambarkan sesuatu memiliki jumlah yang sesuai dengan yang diperlukan.

Penggunaan Enough

1. Enough + noun

Contoh: We have enough milk to make two glasses of milkshake.

2. Adjective/ adverb + enough

Contoh: She is smart enough to teach us.

3. Verb + enough

Contoh: You have prepared enough to join the competition.


29. a block has density of 2500 kg/m^3 and when in air it weight 25 newton. determine the weight of the block in water​


Jawaban:

15 Newton

Penjelasan:

[tex]w = m × g \\

25 = m × 10 \\

m = 2.5 \: kg \\

ρ = \frac{m}{v} \\ 2500 = \frac{2.5}{v} \\ v = {10}^{ - 3} \: {m}^{3}[/tex]

[tex]Fa = \: ρf \times g \times v \: \\ Fa = \: {10}^{3} \times 10 \times {10}^{ - 3} = 10 \: N[/tex]

[tex]w'=w - Fa =25 - 10 = 15 \: N[/tex]

Fa = Archimedes Force

ρf = fluid density (water ρf = 1 000 kg/m³)

w' = weight of the block in water

32. The volume of a cubical block of wood is 2744 ml. Find the length of one edge of the block in cm!


Short answer:

14 cm

Explanations:

1 ml = 1 centimeter cubic = 1 cm³

volume of a cubical block of wood = volume of a cubical

2.744 ml = edge x edge x edge

2.744 cm³ = edge³

edge = [tex]\sqrt[3]{2.744}[/tex] cm

edge = 14 cm

Conclusion: the length of one edge of the block is 14 cm

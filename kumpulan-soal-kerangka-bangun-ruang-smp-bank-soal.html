28. volume bangun ruang di samping adalahdibantu secepatnya y kak soalnya bsk dikumpul​


Jawaban:

volume gabungan

diameter tabung = diameter kerucut = 14 cm

r = d/2 = 14/2 = 7 cm

tinggi tabung = 12 cm, dan tinggi kerucut = (18 - 12) = 6 cm

maka,

= volume tabung + volume kerucut

= (πr²t) + (⅓ × πr²t)

= (22/7 × 7² × 12) + (⅓ × 22/7 × 7² × 6)

= (22/7 × 49 × 12) + (1/3 × 22/7 × 49 × 6)

= (22 × 7 × 12) + (22 × 7 × 2)

= 1.848 + 308

= 2.156 cm³

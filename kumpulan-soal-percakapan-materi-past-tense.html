1. tolong ya soal materi past tense​


Susunlah kata-kata ini agar menjadi benar. Kemudian tulis dalam bentuk kalimat PAST TENSE.

Pembahasan :
Rumus simple PAST nominal adalah
(+) subject + To be (was/were) + complement
(-) subject + To be (was/were) + not + complement
(?) To be (was/were) + subject + complement

Catatan !!!
* To be "was" digunakan apabila subject nya I/she/he/it/orang ketiga tunggal.
* To be "were" digunakan apabila subject nya you/they/we/plural subject.

Jadi, jawabannya adalah :
1). Andra WAS the smartest student in School.
=> Andra adalah murid paling pintar di sekolah.

2). I WAS very sleepy this morning.
=> Saya sangat mengantuk pagi ini.

3). We WERE sorry for him.
=> Kami minta maaf untuknya.

4). She WAS a nice neighbour.
=> Dia tetangga yang baik.

5). The house WAS damage because of the flood.
=> Rumah itu rusak karena banjir.

6). Mother WAS not in Bandung yesterday.
=> Ibu tidak berada di bandung kemarin.

7). The concert WAS fantastic.
=> Konser itu luar biasa.

8). Where WERE you last night?
=> Dimana kamu kemarin malam?

9). John WAS not with Tammy at the movies.
=> John tidak bersama Tammy di bioskop.

10). WAS he late yesterday?
=> Apakah dia terlambat kemarin?

Jawaban :PART A

4). John was cooking the dinner when he burnt his hand.

PART B

1). I was studying English at 08.00 yesterday.

2). I was watching TV at 17.00 p.m last sunday

3). I was having dinner at 19.00 p.m last night.

PART C

1). After I had heard the news, I hurried to see him.

2). Before we went very far, we had found that we had lost our way.

3). I was sorry that I had hurt him.

4). Did you post the letter after you had wrote it?

5). Try not to be absent from class again for the rest of the term. You had already missed too many classes. You missed two classes just last week.

6). He said that he had sent the invitation letter before.

PEMBAHASAN

Simple past adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Verb-2 + O

(-) S + Did + Not + Verb-1 + O

(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + O

Tanda waktu :

YesterdayTwo (weeks) agoLast (year)Three (hours) agoEtc

Contoh :

I bought some books yesterday.I did not buy some books yesterday.Did I buy some books yesterday?

Past continuous adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sedang berlangsung pada masa lampau di saat peristiwa lain terjadi/dilakukan.

Rumus :

(+) S + To be (was/were) + Verb-ing + O

(-) S + To be (was/were) + not + Verb-ing + O

(?) To be (was/were) + S + Verb-ing + O

Tanda waktu :

....when.......as........while...All day yesterdayEtc

Contoh :

She was listening to the radio when I called her.While I was cooking, my sister did her homework.

Past perfect adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan tentang kejadian/perbuatan yang sudah selesai dilakukan pada waktu lampau.

Rumus :

(+) S + Had + Verb-3 + O

(-) S + Had + not + Verb-3 + O

(?) Had + S + Verb-3 + O

Tanda waktu :

....before........after......as soon as.......until...

Contoh :

Before I arrived, The train had left.She told me her name after I had asked her twice.

9. buatlah soal past tense + past perfect (5),past tense + past continous(5)


A. PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINUOUS
1. My mother called me last night, when I was studying
2. The children were watching television, when their parents came home
3. You were making dinner, when my friends went home
4. When she was cleaning the windows, her husband called me
5. I was sleeping, when my brother woke me up

Note :
Gunakan PAST CONTINUOUS pada kalimat atau kejadian yang terjadi lebih dulu. Dalam bahasa Indonesia bisa diartikan "..... SEDANG MELAKUKAN".
Baik PAST TENSE dan PAST CONTINUOUS digunakan untuk kalimat yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

B. PAST TENSE VS PAST PERFECT
1. When she was 21 years old, she had married
2. He had a luxury car, before he was 30 years old
3. They had had lunch, before they went to the library
4. Before we watched television, we had had done our homework
5. After they had gone to Paris, they went to England

Note :
Gunakan PAST PERFECT pada kalimat atau kejadian yang sudah selesai dikerjakan, dan dilanjutkan kejadian selanjutnya dimana kalimatnya menggunakan SIMPLE PAST. Dalam bahasa Indonesia, PAST PERFECT bisa diartikan ".... SUDAH MELAKUKAN".

25. carilah materi tentang simple past tense​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense merupakan tenses yang digunakan untuk membicarakan sesuatu yang umum, sesuatu yang selalu terjadi berulang-ulang, atau suatu kebenaran umum. Simple present tense adalah tenses yang paling sering digunakan sehari-hari.

Untuk mengenali bahwa sebuah kalimat merupakan simple present tense, ada beberapa cara yang bisa dilakukan, salah satunya adalah kalimat tersebut menggunakan kata kerja bentuk dasar (Verb 1). Simple present tense terbagi menjadi dua pola kalimat, yaitu verbal simple present dan nominal simple present tense.

Penjelasan:

Semoga membantu

Jawaban:

Simple past tense digunakan untuk membicarakan suatu kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau dan berakhir pula di masa lampau dalam waktu yang spesifik. Kalimat dalam simple past tense ditandai dengan kata kerja kedua (verb2) yang menunjukkan waktu lampau.

Pola kalimat simple past tense terbagi menjadi 2 rumus, yaitu verbal simple past tense dan nominal simple past tense.

1. Verbal Simple Past

(+)

Subject + Verb2 + Object

(-)

Subject + did + not + verb1

(?)

Did + Subject + Verb1

Pada simple past tense, kata kerja kedua (V2) hanya digunakan pada kalimat positif. Jika bentuk kalimatnya negatif dan pertanyaan, maka akan kembali ke kata kerja dasar pertama dengan menambahkan did sebelum kata kerja.

Contoh kalimat verbal simple past tense:

-My mom and I went to the zoo yesterday

2. Nominal Simple Past

(+)

Subject + to be (was/were) + adjective/adverb

(-)

Subject +to be (was/were) + adjective/adverb

(?)

to be (was/were) + Subject + adjective/adverb

Was digunakan untuk subjek I, She, He, It

Were digunakan untuk subjek You, They, We

Contoh kalimat nominal simple past tense:

I was here last night

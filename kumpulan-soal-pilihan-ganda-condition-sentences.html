7. Format penulisan perulangan do-while yang benar adalah?a) do-while(condition);{//code} b) do{//code}while(condition);c) do-while(condition){//code} d) do{//code}while(condition)​


Halo saya akan menjawab pertanyaan mu!

Format penulisan perulangan do-while yang benar adalah?

a) do-while(condition);{//code}

b) do{//code}while(condition);

c) do-while(condition){//code}

d) do{//code}while(condition)​

Penjelasan

Penulisan struktur do-while mirip dengan struktur while, namun kita menambahkan perintah do di awal struktur. Berikut adalah format dasar penulisan struktur do-while dalam PHP:

do {

   statement;

   statement;

} while (condition);

Setelah perintah do, di dalam blok kurung kurawal adalah statement. Statement adalah kode program yang akan diulang. Kita bisa membuat beberapa kode program untuk menampilkan perintah seperti echo, atau perintah yang lebih kompleks. Namun di dalam bagian ini harus ada baris program yang digunakan sebagai ’penghenti’ perulangan.

Condition adalah kondisi yang harus dipenuhi agar perulangan berlangsung. Selama condition bernilai TRUE, maka perulangan akan terus dilakukan. Condition ini akan diperiksa pada tiap perulangan, dan hanya jika hasilnya FALSE, maka proses perulangan berhenti.

25. Please make three sentences each condition or dialogues by using "what for..... " And "what.......for"​


Penjelasan:

Question words are words we put at the start of a question to ask for particular information. Here they are:

What – to ask about things when there are many possible answers

Which – to ask about things when there are a limited number of possible answers

Who – to ask about people

Where – to ask about place

When – to ask about a date or time

Why – to ask the reason

How – to ask about condition or a method

Here are some examples:

What did you do last weekend? – We went to the seaside.

Which tennis court are we playing on? 1 or 2?

Who’s that? – That’s Mr Roberts.

Where are you from? – I’m from Australia.

When’s your birthday? – It’s on 25th May.

Why do you need to learn English? – It’s important for my job.

How’s your brother? – He’s well thanks.

32. condition sentences, ke type 1 ya kak tolong banget yang mengerti​. terimakasih​


Jawaban:

Conditional type-1

1. If I come come earlier, I will prepare dinner.

2. If I have free time, I will go swimming.

3. What will you do if you miss the plane?

4. If she calls you, you will go.

5. Today will be very cool  if it rains.

Penjelasan:

Conditional type-3 menyatakan pengandaian yang bertitik pada situasi saat ini (saat si pembicara mengucapkan kalimatnya) dan masih bisa terjadi, jadi bukan angan-angan.

Struktur kalimat conditional type-1:

If clause (bagian pengandaian, bagian di mana ad akata -Jika/If):

simple present tense (if + subjek + verb1)

Contoh dalam soal nomor 4, if clause-nya adalah:

If he (call) you....

Maka yang benar sesuai strukturnya adalah:

If he calls you (simple present tense. Verb call ada tambahan s karena subjeknya He, sesuai kaidah simple present tense)

Main clause (bagian yang menjadi akibat/konsekwensi dari pengandaian)

bentuk tensenya adalah simpple future tense" (subjek + will + verb1)

Contoh soal 4, main clause nya adalah: You go

Jadi sesuai kaidahnya, kita buat ke simple future: You will go.

Makna kalimat itu dalam bahasa Indonesia: Jika dia menelpon kamu, maka kamu akan pergi.

Semoga bermanfaat

One of the main causes of pollution in my city is the high number of vehicles on the roads. Every day, thousands of cars, buses, and motorcycles clog the streets, emitting exhaust fumes and other pollutants into the air. This has a significant impact on the quality of the air that we breathe.

Another major cause of pollution is industrial activities. Many factories in the city discharge their waste and chemicals into the water and air, causing significant harm to the environment and public health.

The effects of pollution are widespread and serious. Air pollution can cause respiratory problems, heart disease, and cancer, while water pollution can lead to the contamination of drinking water and the destruction of natural habitats. In addition, pollution can also have a negative impact on the economy, as it reduces the attractiveness of the city as a place to live and do business.

To address this problem, it is essential that we take action to reduce the sources of pollution and improve the quality of our environment. This could include measures such as promoting the use of public transportation, regulating industrial activities, and increasing public awareness about the importance of protecting the environment.

By taking these steps, we can help to reduce the negative effects of pollution and create a healthier and more sustainable future for ourselves and future generations.

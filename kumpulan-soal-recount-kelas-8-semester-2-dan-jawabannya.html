5. soal dan jawaban recount text dalam bahas inggris


Contoh soal recount text

Last weekend, I visited my pen pal's house. Her name is Rosie. There were many activities I did there. In the morning, Rosie and I had breakfast. We had traditional food. I liked it very much.

After we had breakfast, Rosie took me to the garden behind her house. The garden was very big and beautiful. I spent a long time feeding the birds. I also took pictures with those beautiful birds.

After visiting the bird cage, Rosie and I went to the flower garden not far from her house. We took a rest and had lunch under a big tree and watch butterflies flying above colorful flowers. In the afternoon, we swam in the pool in the backyard. It was so fun. I really enjoyed my time with Rosie.

1. The text above tells us about...

a. a holiday at a friend's place.

b. a picture of a bird.

c. a big bird cage.

d. a big garden

2. What is the last paragraph about?

a. Rosie had butterflies as her pet.

b. The writer's friend is a good swimmer.

c. There are a lot of flowers in Rosie's house.

d. The writer had a good time with her friend.

3. Where did the writer spend her afternoon?

a. Under a tree.

b. In the swimming pool.

c. In the flower garden.

d. Inside the big bird cage.

4. From the text, we know that the writer...

a. Had gone and visited many places during her holiday.

b. Lived in the same village with her pen pal, Rosie.

c. Spent her holiday at a friend's house.

d. Liked butterflies and swimming very much.

Pembahasan

Recount text merupakan salah satu jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang berisi mengenai cerita tentang kegiatan penulis berupa pengalaman yang dialami penulis. Tujuan penulisan recount text yakni memberikan informasi tentang pengalaman penulis sekaligus menghibur pembaca.

Recount text memiliki generic structure sebagai berikut:

Orientation, berisi informasi umum mengenai tokoh, tempat kejadian, kapan kejadian berlangsung dan lain sebagainya.Events, merupakan isi dari teks berupa cerita mengenai pengalaman yang ingin disampaikan oleh penulis kepada pembaca.Reorientation, merupakan bagian penutup yang berisi kesimpulan dari kegiatan penulis.

32. Recount text itu kayak gimana ?? kelas 8 !!


recount text bisa diartikan sebagai "Text yang menceritakan"
Generic structure (susunan umum) recount text ini terdiri dari: orientation, events, reorientation.
Contoh Recount Text (1) tentang Masa SMP
My Adolescence
I had my adolescence when I was thirteen.
It started with acne that showed up on my face. It was very annoying. It lowered my self-esteem and I was embarrassed to come out of my house and play with friends. Fortunately, my Mum gave me a good medicine. In three weeks, the acnes started to vanish although those showed some black spots in my face.
That was my bad experience with adolescence, though there were still lots of good experience too.
Analisa :
Orientation : Paragraf pertama, (I had my adolescence when I was thirteen) Events : Paragraf kedua dan ketiga. Reorientation : Paragraf keempat (terakhir)


Recount text itu untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian di masa lampau,information (lampau), atau pengalaman.
Karena masa lampau jadi menggunakan tenses:Simple past, Past continuous, atau Past perfect.

Recount text adalah salah satu jenis teks dalam Bahasa Inggris yang bercerita tentang kegiatan atau tindakan yang dilakukan oleh tokoh-tokoh dalam cerita tersebut. Biasanya, recount text ditulis dengan tujuan untuk memberikan informasi sekaligus menghibur pembaca.

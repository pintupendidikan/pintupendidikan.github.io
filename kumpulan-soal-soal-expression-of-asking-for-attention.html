3. expression of asking for attention artinya​


Jawaban:

Expression of asking for attention berarti ekspresi untuk meminta perhatian. Ekspresi ini biasanya digunakan untuk menarik perhatian seseorang atau sekelompok orang sebelum menyampaikan informasi atau gagasan penting.

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh ekspresi dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk meminta perhatian:

1. "Excuse me, everyone, can I have your attention, please?"

2. "Could I have your attention for a moment, please?"

3. "May I have your attention, please?"

4. "Could you please listen to me for a second?"

5. "I would appreciate it if everyone could focus on what I am saying."

6. "Can everybody look this way, please?"

21. 5 SOAL TENTANG EXPRESSIONS FOR ASKING ATTENTION


Expressing of Asking attention

When you have to deliver announcement in front of class you need to express the expression to make you become the centre of attention. Below are some common expression used to make the class listen to your speech or announcement. 

1. May I have your attention, please 
2. Can I get the attention! Thanks 
3. Excuse me! 
4. Attention please! 
5. Stop talking please… 
6. Everybody, may I have your attention, please ?
